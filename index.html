Body Mass Index

What is Body Mass Index ?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value derived from a person's weight and height, commonly used to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight relative to their height. It is a simple screening tool to classify individuals into weight categories, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity.

BMI Categories

BMI < 18.5: Underweight.
BMI 18.5 – 24.9: Normal weight.
BMI 25 – 29.9: Overweight.
BMI ≥ 30: Obese.
